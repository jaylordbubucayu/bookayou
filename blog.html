Hope this can help you a lot, Here are five ways you can enjoy your Departamental Day Celebration.

1. Start the day with a prayer

Start with a prayer gives to a positive vibes, Starting each day with a prayer, As God said in His word "In the morning, O Lord, I will direct my song and my prayer unto You and will look up. (Psalm 5:3).

As we start each day, we should seek His presence through prayer. For Him to be able to guide us, protect us, and give us what we need in that day. Prayer makes us see impossible things possible. Means that your celebration will worth it and unforgetable.

2. Forget your academics

Remember the celebration is not just for the department, but it is for you.have a peacefull mindset that you will not do anything but to enjoy. enjoy every moments with your friends or love one (as if)lol!.

3. Participate any contest

join any contest before this is the chance to purchase your talents, and also to discover your hidden talent.

4. Watch your friends and with your friends

Watching your friend participation/performance is giving your all out support so your friend will gain confidence to do all his/her best. And Encourage Friends to do great in every step of the contest.
Dont forget to take some photos!

Support is not just showing up because support really means is showing up when they didn't even need your help. Reminding you are always there at their side giving a hand without any favor or consequence. You want to be the one pushing your friends to be the best version of them they can be. Don't push too hard as they have enough pressure on them as it is and do your job.

5. Rest , Reimagine, Recap

Recall your activities you've done in whole day make you happy in everytime. Because all memories are permanent, and it would be the happy pill or motivation do to such thing.
